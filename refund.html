Refund Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

This Refund Policy applies to Globeii and all related services.

Free & Paid Plans

Globeii offers a free version with limited features. Paid subscription plans unlock additional features and functionality.

Subscriptions

Paid subscriptions are available on a monthly or yearly basis and automatically renew unless canceled before the renewal date.

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Canceling prevents future charges. Access to paid features will remain available until the end of the current billing period.

Refunds

All fees are non-refundable. We do not provide refunds or credits for partial billing periods, unused time, or unused features.

Refunds may only be issued where required by applicable law.

Account Deletion

Deleting your Globeii account automatically cancels any active subscriptions. No further charges will occur. Access continues until the end of the billing period.

Contact

If you believe you were charged in error, please contact us at: support@globeii.app
