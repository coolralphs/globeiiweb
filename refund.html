Refund Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2026

Globeii is operated by Globeii, LLC. This Refund Policy explains our policies regarding subscription payments, cancellations, and refunds.

1. Free Version

Globeii offers a free version of the Services. No payment is required, and no refunds apply to free features.

2. Paid Subscriptions

Globeii offers monthly and yearly subscription plans through our web application. Subscriptions automatically renew unless canceled before the renewal date.

2.1 Cancellation

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time.
  • Canceling a subscription stops future billing.
  • Access to paid features continues until the end of the current billing period.

2.2 Refunds

  • All subscription payments are non-refundable unless required by law.
  • We generally do not issue refunds for partially used billing periods or accidental purchases.
  • For billing errors or disputes, please contact us at support@globeii.app.

3. Free Trials & Upgrades

If you upgrade from the free plan to a paid subscription, you will be charged immediately. Refunds for upgrades or trial conversions are generally not provided, except as required by law.

4. Enterprise / Team Plans

Enterprise or team subscriptions are billed to the organization or account owner. Fees are based on the chosen plan and number of users.

  • Failure to manage team access does not entitle the account holder to refunds.
  • Adjustments to team subscriptions (adding/removing users) are handled per plan terms; see your plan agreement for details.

5. Account Deletion

Deleting your account immediately cancels any active subscription. You will not be charged further. Access ends immediately upon deletion, and no refunds are issued for the remaining billing period.

6. Offline Data

Any itineraries or other data downloaded to your device for offline use are your responsibility. Globeii is not liable for offline data loss or corruption. Deleting offline data is permanent.

7. Third-Party Payments

All payments are processed by third-party payment providers. We are not responsible for issues related to payment processing, card declines, chargebacks, or other payment errors. Refunds may require coordination with the payment provider.
